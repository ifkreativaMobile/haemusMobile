HAEMUS

Center for scientific research and
promotion of culture

We would like to introduce briefly our organization. We are founded in 2012 and until today we’ve grown up as the only organization of this kind in the Balkans. HAEMUS strives to promote scientific research and culture on the Balkan Peninsula, offering a wide range cultural heritage services. These fall into several categories, including: research, cultural resource management, publication, education, promotion and tourism.
